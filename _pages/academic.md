I am an Assistant Professor in AI and NLP for Healthcare at UMC Utrecht. My research combines clinically grounded machine learning with practical deployment, focusing on language models, trustworthy automation, and data-driven support for patient care.

## Education

- BSc, Life Science and Technology, TU Delft and Leiden University, 2011-2015
- MSc, Biomedical Engineering, TU Delft, 2015-2017
- PhD, Image Analysis for Orthopedics, UMC Utrecht, 2017-2022

## Career

- PhD Researcher, UMC Utrecht, 2017-2022
- Technical Lead, Presurgeo, 2021-2022
- Senior Specialist Data Science, Valcon, 2023-2024
- Assistant Professor AI and NLP, Julius Center UMC Utrecht, 2024-present

## Research themes

- Clinical validation of LLM-assisted workflows
- AI for medical documentation and care process support
- Methods at the intersection of NLP and medical image analysis
