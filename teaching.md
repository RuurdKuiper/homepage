# Teaching

My teaching focuses on practical AI methods for healthcare, with emphasis on responsible model use,
clear evaluation, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

## Courses

- Medische Beeldvormende Technieken (2019-2021)
- Missing Data, practical sessions (2024)
- Big Data course, LLM practical (2025)
- Machine Learning in Healthcare (2025)

## Teaching Style

- Hands-on assignments with realistic clinical data scenarios
- Reproducible workflows and transparent model evaluation
- Communication across clinical and technical disciplines

## Student Supervision

I supervise student projects in AI for healthcare and medical NLP.
If you are interested in a thesis topic, please reach out with your background and interests.
